DESIGN

At Vizora, we believe good design is more than just looks—it’s about making websites that are clean, simple, and easy to use.
In this course, you’ll learn the basics of layout, color theory, typography, and user experience.
Whether you’re starting from zero or want to improve your style, this section gives you the foundation you need to create designs that stand out.

  • Understand layout principles for clean, user-friendly pages
  • Learn color theory to create visually appealing designs
  • Master typography for better readability and branding
  • Explore user experience (UX) fundamentals
  • Apply design principles through real-world examples
